About

Two disciplines.
One eye.

Kristian Eustace is a photographer and creative director based in Europe. Trained as a precision engineer before stepping in front of — and behind — the lens professionally, he brings a rigour to image-making that most photographers never develop.

Working across branding, portraiture, and editorial, Kristian has spent years on set across Thailand, Athens, and Milan. The precision that defined his engineering career never left. It simply found a new subject.
